About Animal Disease Advisor

This web-based Knowledge-Based System (KBS) helps livestock farmers diagnose common diseases in cattle and goats based on visible symptoms.

The system uses AI principles like rule-based inference to suggest likely diseases and basic care advice before a veterinarian is contacted.
